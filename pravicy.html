Privacy Policy

TOURISTS GALORE is Pakistan’s premium travel platform providing free listing of travel products for travel agents in Pakistan. Across the website, we collect user information in different ways and this document explains on how we collect and use that data and the associated security and privacy parameters.

Collection of Data

On the website, we collect users’ personal information such as name, contact number, email address, postal address and profile photo. All this information is stored on our dedicated servers which is located in Pakistan.

How We Save Your Data?

Your passwords and sensitive information are saved in our database in encrypted format.

How Secure is Your Data?

We implement standard security protocols such as HTTPS during data transfer, keep sensitive data in encrypted format and applying all fixes and patches on the server in due time.

How We Use Your Data?

We don’t share your data to any marketing companies. However, we have verified travel partners who might get access to certain data elements. Also, we give you possibility to delete your account and all of its data by sending us an email on touristsgaloreoffical@gmail.com

Use of Cookies

We do save cookies on your computer/device when you login to improve your website and app experience.

TOURISTS GALORE Official Standing

We want to make it very clear that TOURISTS GALORE do not represent or claim to affiliate with any of the government organization/department or any travel regulator. We are a private entity. All the information provided on the website and our app is user generated content and moderated by our in-house experts. We provide a login system to travel organizations which post their content on the platform. Also some content like reviews and comments are also posted by the independent users which later is moderated by our team.

Have More Questions? Contact Us

You can always contact us for any general queries or questions about your privacy of data at touristsgaloreoffical@gmail.com
